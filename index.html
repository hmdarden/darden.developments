Connecting the right message with the right audience.

Hi, I’m Hannah! I bring together digital strategy, audience insight, and clear communication to help messages reach the right people and drive meaningful action. Whether I’m optimizing campaigns, analyzing performance, shaping content, or improving user experiences, my focus is on making communication more intentional, accessible, and effective.

What I Work With

My toolkit includes Meta Ads Manager, Google Ads, Google Analytics, Figma, Adobe Creative Cloud, WordPress, HTML/CSS, and collaborative platforms like Confluence, Asana, and Monday.com. I use these tools to support campaign planning, audience targeting, performance reporting, and digital content strategy. But more than the tools themselves, my real strengths are connecting messages with the right audiences, turning data into clear recommendations, and shaping communication that helps people understand what to do next.

My Foundation

I recently completed my MA in Professional and Technical Writing at the University of Arkansas at Little Rock, with a focus on user experience, digital content strategy, and audience research. I also hold a BA in Web Design & Development, along with certificates in Digital Marketing, Google Analytics, and Google Project Management. Together, my background in UX, web, analytics, and communication helps me approach digital strategy with both audience understanding and performance in mind.

Outside the Screen

When I’m not working, you’ll find me reading sci-fi/fantasy or in a studio metalsmithing, doing stained glass, or sculpting. For me, physical design is another way to practice problem-solving and making things clearer, more beautiful, and more useful.
