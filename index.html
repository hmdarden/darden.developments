About

    • What I Work With

    My toolkit includes Figma, Adobe Creative Cloud, Camtasia, HTML/CSS, JavaScript and WordPress — plus collaborative tools like Confluence and Monday.com. But more than any software, my real strengths are designing intuitive experiences, writing clear content and making sure users always come first.

    • How I'm Growing

    I'm currently pursuing my MA in Professional and Techincal Writing at the University of Arkansas - Little Rock, focusing my studies on User Experience Design. I hold a BA in Web Design & Development from the University of Arkansas - Little Rock. I've also completed the Google Project Management Certificate program.

    • Outside the Screen

    When I’m not working, you’ll usually find me lost in a good sci-fi or fantasy story — or in my studio with a torch or soldering iron in hand. I love metalsmithing, stained glass art, and sculpting because they challenge me to think through design problems step by step, just like in UX. Whether I’m building something physical or digital, I’m happiest when I’m making things clearer, more beautiful, and more useful for people.
